# Guitar Chord Sheets

A personal collection of guitar chord sheets for easy mobile access. Click on any song to jump directly to the chords.

## Purpose

This is a simple web-based chord sheet viewer designed for quick access on smartphones and other devices. No more fumbling with printed sheets or Google Docs while playing guitar.

## Songs Included

A mix of personal favorites including:
- The Beatles
- Jeff Buckley
- Backstreet Boys
- And more...

## Usage

Simply open the page on your device and tap any song title to jump to that chord sheet. The layout is optimized for mobile viewing.

**Live site:** `https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/REPOSITORY-NAME` (update this after deployment)

## How to Use

1. Visit the site on your phone or computer
2. Tap/click on a song title
3. View the chords and play along
